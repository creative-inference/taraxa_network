Autonomous AI Agents: The Future of On-Chain Activity

AI Agents are becoming first-class citizens in the Web3 economy. Taraxa equips these agents with identity, assets, and the ability to transact, creating what can be described as 'GPTs with wallets.'

What are AI Agents in this Context?

These are software programs powered by artificial intelligence that can perform tasks autonomously. On Taraxa, they can be registered with a unique on-chain identity, possess a wallet to hold and transact assets, and interact with smart contracts and other agents.

DeFAI: Decentralized Financial AI

A key application is DeFAI, where AI agents autonomously manage liquidity, optimize yield farming strategies, participate in DAO governance by voting, and execute complex financial transactions. They analyze vast amounts of real-time data to make informed decisions, far exceeding human capacity for certain tasks.

Taraxa's Enabling Features:

  • Machine ID: Provides verifiable identities for AI agents.
  • High Throughput & Low Cost: Allows agents to perform frequent, complex operations economically.
  • Smart Contract Interaction: Agents can trigger, deploy, and interact with 'Machine Agreements'.
  • Data Integration: Agents can pull on-chain and off-chain data (via oracles) and push actions back to various systems.

The Agentic Economy

This leads to an 'agentic economy' where a significant portion of economic activity is driven by autonomous AI agents, operating independently of direct human intervention, fostering new levels of efficiency and complexity.
