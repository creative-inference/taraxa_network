Unlocking Value through Tokenization

Tokenization represents physical or digital assets as transferable digital tokens on a blockchain. Taraxa enables robust and efficient tokenization, particularly for assets within the machine economy.

What is Tokenization?

It's the process of converting rights to an asset into a digital token on a blockchain. These tokens can represent ownership, usage rights, or a share in future revenue.

On Taraxa, this means machines can have tokenized 'shares' representing their economic output or operational capacity, allowing for decentralized ownership and investment.

Benefits with Taraxa

  • Increased Liquidity: Fractional ownership and easier transfer of machine assets.
  • Transparency: Ownership records are immutable and verifiable on-chain.
  • New Investment Models: Enables community funding for DePIN projects and individual machines.
  • Machine Capital Formation: Machines can 'own' parts of other machines or services.

Example: Robot Shares

Imagine a fleet of autonomous delivery robots. Each robot's potential earning power can be tokenized. Individuals or other machines can buy these 'Robot Shares', funding the robot's acquisition or maintenance, and in return, receive a portion of its earnings.
