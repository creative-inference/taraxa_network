Understanding Machine Agreements

Machine Agreements are the next evolution of smart contracts, specifically designed for autonomous M2M interactions...

What are they?

They are self-executing contracts with the terms of the agreement directly written into code. For machines, this means automated enforcement, trustless execution, and transparent logging of all interactions.

Taraxa's architecture allows these agreements to be highly efficient and cost-effective.

Use Cases

  • Automated supply chain logistics
  • Robotic fleet management
  • Decentralized energy grids
