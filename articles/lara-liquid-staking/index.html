Lara Liquid Staking

LARA is the native liquid staking protocol on the Taraxa blockchain, designed to simplify staking, enhance DeFi participation, and maintain token liquidity.


Purpose and Functionality

LARA is a decentralized liquid staking solution that allows users to stake TARA tokens while receiving stTARA tokens (staked TARA) in a 1:1 ratio. These stTARA tokens remain liquid, enabling users to participate in DeFi applications like liquidity provision on decentralized exchanges (DEXes) or other protocols while still earning staking rewards.



Automated Staking and Delegation

LARA automates the staking and delegation process, allowing users to stake TARA tokens and delegate them to validators with a single click. It eliminates the need for manual management and KYC, making staking accessible and user-friendly.


Auto-Compounding Rewards

The protocol features auto-compounding, which reinvests staking rewards to maximize returns without user intervention. Users can opt in or out of this feature at any time, offering flexibility.


Liquidity for Staked Assets

By issuing stTARA tokens, LARA ensures that staked TARA remains liquid, allowing users to use these tokens across Taraxa’s DeFi ecosystem, such as in lending or trading, without sacrificing staking rewards.
